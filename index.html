Welcome to Rajdhani Sweets & Restaurant

Rajdhani Sweets is one of the best Indian Vegetarian Food Restaurant Chains in Canada offering Desi Sweets and Snacks that please your sweet tooth and taste buds at affordable prices.

Your hunt for the best and delicious North Indian Sweets/Snacks and South Indian Tiffins ends here. Rajdhani Sweets proudly boasts of making delicious Indian sweets and snacks!

We offer authentic desi flavors that list below:

Snacks: Samosa Chana, Chat Papri, Pani Puri, Aloo Tikki Chana, Bhel Puri, Dahi Bhalla, Pakora, and more.

Bread: Plain Naan, Garlin Naan, Onion Naan, Aloo Naan, Roti Naan, Paratha, and more.

South Indian: Idli Sambar, Vada Sambar, Masala Dosa, Plain Dosa and more.

Main Course:Veg. Biryani, Chana Bhatura, Makki Roti Saag, Chana Kulcha, Paratha Dahi, Halwa Puri, Lacha Paratha & Dal Tadka, and more.

Desserts: Gulab Jamun, Ras Malai, Kulfi Faluda, Moong Dal Halwa, Raj Rabri, and more.

Drinks: Sweet/Salt Lassi, Milk Badam, Mango Shake, Coffee, Tea, and more.

Sweets: Kaju Burfi, Koya Burfi, Motichoor laddu, Pink chum chum, Dry Fruit Roll, kalakand, Gujia, Milk Cake, and more.

We are committed to serving and delivering better quality Indian Vegetarian Food that includes North & South Indian cuisine, Snacks, Desserts, and more at the best price.

Order or buy Indian sweets from the reputed Indian Sweet House cum Restaurant, RajdhaniSweets - Sweets & Snacks with a blend of desi Pyaar…..!
